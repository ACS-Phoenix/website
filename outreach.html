Outreach

From captivating children at Muzeiko with the wonders of robotics to showcasing innovative technologies at the Industrial Tech Forum, our team has been at the forefront of introducing the Phoenix robot to a wide range of individuals. Through participation in events such as RoboDays and Hello Space, we've shared our passion for robotics with enthusiasts, professionals, and space exploration enthusiasts alike. Each event has provided a unique opportunity to demonstrate the capabilities and potential of our creation, fostering curiosity and excitement wherever we go.

Outreach is the heartbeat of our FIRST Robotics team

It's about building connections, sharing the marvels of STEM, and leaving an unforgettable impression.
  • ACS Phoenix had the privilege of showcasing their robot at Muzeiko, an innovative children's science center.
    November 4, 2023
  • ACS Phoenix was also a part of cinaugural Industrial Tech Forum in Sofia, contributing to the showcase of cutting-edge technological achievements.
    October 4, 2023
  • ACS Phoenix proudly participated in Robo Days, an annual event organized by the Robotics Club at the Technical University in Sofia.
    April 29, 2023
  • The Bulgarian youth STEAM & Space festival HELLO SPACE was a great opportunity for the ACS Phoenix to present its accomplishments and aspire the new generation.
    June 29, 2023

We are on a mission to promote the concept of Robotics throughout Bulgaria.

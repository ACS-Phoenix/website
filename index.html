Our motivation

What makes us move forward

ACS Phoenix (9089) was founded in 2022, inspired by the opportunity to participate in FIRST and united by a passion for creativity and engineering. Several dedicated and hardworking enthusiasts of robotics came together to initiate and drive the club's activities
Our mission goes beyond crafting impressive robots; we're driven to positively impact our community. We're not just pursuing our interests; our goal is to ignite excitement about science and technology among students across Bulgaria 🇧🇬
